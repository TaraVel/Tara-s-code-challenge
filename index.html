About

I'm an outgoing, optimistic and friendly girl who loves to travel, meet new people and to learn new things. I like real conversations, practicing yoga and I enjoy a good cup of coffee.

Meet Tara

My background

I'm a graduate of Bachelor of Arts in European Studies. During my studies I took several courses at the College of Business and Management at RMIT University, Melbourne, Australia.

After graduating in 2015 I started working as a Jr. Marketeer at Balanzs. When I got offered a part-time job in the Caribbean, I didn't know what to do. On the one hand I wanted to continue working at Balanzs because I really liked my job, but on the other hand... The Caribbean!

If only there was a way to combine both jobs... And there was. I moved to Bonaire while still working flex hours for Balanzs. It was the best of both worlds.

After a couple of months of living the island life I came back to The Netherlands and started working at the actual office again, instead of working from my hammock on a tropical white beach. When I got promoted to Jr. Marketing Manager at work, I figured it was about time to work better, smarter, more efficient. Hence, my motivation to join the Growth Tribe.
